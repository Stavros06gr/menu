Transmission System

The transmission system transfers power from the engine to the driveshaft, allowing the vehicle to change speed and torque. It includes the gearbox, clutch, torque converter, and gear sets.

How Transmission Works

  • Gearbox: Adjusts torque and speed through gear ratios.
  • Clutch: Connects and disconnects engine power to the transmission.
  • Torque Converter: Transfers rotating power in automatic transmissions.
  • Gear Sets: Different gears that provide various speed and torque options.

Key Components

  • Gearbox: Contains the gears that change speed and torque.
  • Clutch: Engages and disengages power transmission.
  • Torque Converter: Fluid coupling that transmits power in automatic transmissions.
  • Shift Linkage: Connects the gear selector to the transmission.
