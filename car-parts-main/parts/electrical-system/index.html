Electrical System

The electrical system powers the vehicle's electronics, lights, ignition, and charging components. It includes the battery, alternator, starter motor, and wiring.

Electrical Function

  • Battery: Supplies power to start the engine and run electronics.
  • Alternator: Charges the battery and powers systems while the engine runs.
  • Starter Motor: Cranks the engine to start the vehicle.
  • Wiring: Connects and delivers power throughout the vehicle.

Key Components

  • Battery: Main source of electrical energy.
  • Alternator: Generates electricity while the engine runs.
  • Starter Motor: Initiates engine ignition.
  • Fuses & Relays: Protect and control electrical circuits.
