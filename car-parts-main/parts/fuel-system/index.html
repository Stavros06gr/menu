Fuel System

The fuel system stores and delivers fuel to the engine for combustion. It includes components like the fuel tank, fuel pump, fuel injectors or carburetor, and fuel filter.

Fuel Delivery Process

  • Fuel Tank: Stores fuel for the engine.
  • Fuel Pump: Sends fuel from the tank to the engine.
  • Fuel Filter: Removes impurities from the fuel.
  • Fuel Injectors/Carburetor: Mix fuel with air for combustion.

Key Components

  • Fuel Tank: Holds gasoline or diesel fuel.
  • Fuel Pump: Provides the required pressure to move fuel.
  • Fuel Filter: Ensures clean fuel reaches the engine.
  • Fuel Injectors: Precisely deliver fuel into the combustion chamber.
